### Temperature (0.0 - 2.0)
- **0.0 - 0.3:** Conservative, predictable, factual
  - *"The conference will be held in Brussels during March 2025."*
- **0.4 - 0.7:** Balanced creativity and reliability
  - *"Join us in the vibrant city of Brussels this March for an unforgettable conference experience."*
- **0.8 - 2.0:** Creative, varied, sometimes unpredictable
  - *"Brussels awaits! Immerse yourself in a transformative March gathering where innovation meets tradition."*

### Top-p (Nucleus Sampling)
- **Low (0.1):** Very focused vocabulary
- **High (0.9):** Diverse word choices

**Business Impact:** Choose parameters based on your use case!
